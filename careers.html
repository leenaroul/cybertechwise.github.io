Cybersecurity Career Paths

Building a career in cybersecurity can feel overwhelming with so many different vendors and training providers out there. To make this easy to navigate, here is a descriptive guide broken down by key organizations, explaining exactly what they offer and which career paths they serve.

Cloud Providers (AWS & Azure)

Cloud security is a must-know because almost every company hosts data online now.

  • AWS (Amazon Web Services): Offers certifications like AWS Certified Security – Specialty. Great for AppSec (Application Security), Cloud SOC Analysts, and Cloud Architecture.
  • Azure (Microsoft): Offers the AZ-500 (Microsoft Azure Security Technologies) and SC-200 (Microsoft Security Operations Analyst). SC-200 is specifically a golden ticket for a cloud-focused SOC Analyst.

Foundational & Broad Training (CompTIA & Universities)

Excellent starting points for constructing structured deep infrastructure knowledge boundaries.

  • CompTIA: The ultimate starting point.
    • Security+: The standard entry-level cert for getting a Cyber Intern job or a junior SOC Analyst role.
    • CySA+ (Cybersecurity Analyst): Ideal for an advanced SOC Analyst or Blue Team member.
    • PenTest+: Introduction to Red Team / Ethical Hacking.
  • College/University: Best for building deep fundamental knowledge (networking, operating systems, programming) and securing structured Cyber Internships. A degree isn't always mandatory, but it opens doors for GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance) and corporate tracks.

Defensive & Security Operations (Blue Team & SOC)

Tailored options for professionals dedicated to threat validation, scanning, monitoring, and live handling analysis.

  • Security Blue Team: A highly practical provider focused purely on defense.
    • BTL1 (Blue Team Level 1): One of the best, most hands-on certifications for becoming a SOC Analyst or Blue Team defender. It skips the multiple-choice questions and tests you in a real digital lab.
  • GIAC (SANS): The absolute gold standard in corporate training, though very expensive.
    • GSEC / GCIA / GCIH: High-end certs for SOC Analysts, Incident Responders (Blue Team), and Purple Team professionals who need to detect advanced threats.

Offensive Security & Ethical Hacking (Red Team)

Techniques focused on finding vulnerabilities, system boundaries penetration testing, and breaking infrastructures.

  • OffSec: Famous for notoriously difficult, hands-on exams.
    • OSCP (Offensive Security Certified Professional): The industry benchmark for penetration testers and Red Team operators.
  • Hack The Box (HTB) & TryHackMe: Interactive gamified learning platforms.
    • TryHackMe: Perfect for absolute beginners and Cyber Interns. They have learning paths dedicated to SOC, Pre-Security, and Junior Penetration Testing.
    • Hack The Box (HTB): Offers the CPTS (Certified Penetration Testing Specialist). HTB is great for intermediate to advanced Red Team and Purple Team learning.
  • TCM Security: Affordable, high-quality video courses.
    • PNPT (Practical Network Penetration Tester): An excellent, budget-friendly certification for breaking into a Red Team career.
  • ZeroPoint Security: Highly specialized training.
    • CRTO (Certified Red Team Operator): The premier course for learning advanced infrastructure attacking, crucial for mature Red Team jobs.
  • CREST: An international certification body. Getting a CREST certification (like CREST Registered Tester) is highly prized in Europe and Asia for corporate Red Team compliance.

Web Application Security (AppSec)

Focused completely on engineering logic flaws, validation problems, and hunting vulnerabilities in applications.

  • PortSwigger: The creators of Burp Suite (the primary tool used to hack web apps). They offer the Web Security Academy, which is 100% free and arguably the best learning platform in the world for AppSec Team paths.

Purple Team & Specialized Labs (INE & EC-Council)

Bridging training boundaries between offensive attacks and defensive responses.

  • INE Security (eLearnSecurity): Offers certifications like eJPT (Junior Penetration Tester) which is great for beginners/interns, and eCPPT for Red Team.
  • EC-Council: Known globally for the CEH (Certified Ethical Hacker). While criticized by some hands-on experts for being purely multiple-choice, it is still highly requested by HR departments and government jobs for Red Team and SOC Analysts.

Management, GRC, and Compliance (ISACA & ISC2)

If you want to work away from the keyboard handling policies, frameworks, laws, and risk, these two are your destination.

  • ISACA:
    • CRISC (Risk) & CISM (Security Management): Tailor-made for GRC Team and Compliance Team tracks.
    • CISA (Certified Information Systems Auditor): The absolute requirement for IT auditors and Compliance roles.
  • ISC2:
    • Certified in Cybersecurity (CC): A free entry-level certification great for a Cyber Intern job.
    • CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional): The most powerful certification in the entire industry for management, high-level GRC, and advanced engineering. (Requires 5 years of experience).
